Our Stories

Noisy Atom have been active in a range of projects since inception in 2014. Started from 3 like minded R&D engineers from the Vodafone Global R&D Centre in Newbury. Our skills are broad and range from IoT, Network Telecomms and web development. We even support OS projects used by UK Government. Have a read at some of our recent stories below.

Office Of National Statistics

Noisy Atom took the lead role in implementing and customization on a python OS OAuth2 server. We now maintain this OS project and actively developing it. You can find more info on this project here

Thermofisher Scientific

AlfaAesar, a sub brand of Thermofisher used Noisy Atom expertise to help port a legacy Websmart system onto a Django ecommerce platform. The system used an asynchronous message bus, elastic search and interfaced to a legacy IBM DB2 database.

Vodafone

Vodafone required a dynamic mapping system for their patent wall at the Vodafone R&D offices in Newbury. A django web portal dynamically mapped a QR code scanned from the patent wall with a phone to details about the patent and inventor.
